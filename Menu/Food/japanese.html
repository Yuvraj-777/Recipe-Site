Japanese Menu

レシピを楽しんでください！
(Reshipi o tanoshinde kudasai!)


Shushi

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Fresh fish (tuna, salmon, etc.)
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Vegetables (cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger (for serving)

    Recipe:

  1. Cook the rice: Rinse and cook sushi rice. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold into the cooked rice.
  2. Prepare the fillings: Slice fish and vegetables into thin strips.
  3. Assemble sushi rolls: Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly over it, and place fish and vegetables in the center. Roll it tightly using the mat and slice.
  4. Serve: Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Ramen

    Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken or pork broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • Sliced pork belly (chashu)
  • Green onions, nori, and bamboo shoots (for garnish)

    Recipe:

  1. Make the broth: Heat chicken or pork broth, mix in soy sauce, miso, and sesame oil, then simmer.
  2. Cook noodles: Boil the ramen noodles in water according to the package instructions.
  3. Assemble the bowl: Place cooked noodles in bowls, pour hot broth over them, and top with pork, soft-boiled eggs, green onions, and other garnishes.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with additional toppings like nori or chili oil.

Tempura

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Shrimp, sweet potatoes, zucchini (for frying)
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Dipping sauce (soy sauce, dashi, mirin)

    Recipe:

  1. Prepare the batter: Beat the egg and mix with cold water. Gradually whisk in flour, leaving the batter slightly lumpy.
  2. Prepare ingredients: Peel and devein shrimp; slice vegetables thinly.
  3. Fry: Heat oil in a pot. Dip shrimp and vegetables in the batter, then fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve: Serve with a light dipping sauce made from soy sauce, dashi, and mirin.
